Start The Conductor Lifecycle


Configure your cloud environment

Conductor has very low integration dependencies. All that is needed is an AWS account along with credentials for any cloud or service provider you care to use with Conductor. You set the permissions, we'll manage the rest.

Enable your desired solutions

The solution gallery contains out of the box functionality for addressing various cloud management problems. Simply click to enable ones you wish to deploy and use. Gain value immediately or fine-tune solution provided defaults later on.

Carve out your cloud organisational model

Conductors query language and resource group support can form any resource hierarchicy you choose. Resource groups show perspectives into how your cloud estate works and lets you manage resources by BU structure, projects, compliance state or any model you choose.

Tune your
policies

Beyond the default policy posture of enabled solutions exist unlimited degrees of freedom in policy management for your Organisation. Control and apply policies in any way you wish and track feedback via Conductors dashboard and resource views.

Create your cloud crescendo

Using our SDK or API's Conductor value can be maximised even further to address problems not yet solved by out of the box functionality. Develop your own solutions and integrations quickly using a well defined methodology or enable third-party solutions hosted on GitHub!
